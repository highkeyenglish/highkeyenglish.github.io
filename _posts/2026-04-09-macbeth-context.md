## Teaching Materials
These materials can be tailored to students of any ability level. It has been designed for tuition (focusing on individual students) but can be adapted for a classroom setting if needed. 

During this lesson. students will:

<ul>
    <li>Become acquainted with the various definitions of context</li>
    <li>Explore the context of Shakespeare's time</li>
    <li>Learn about the context of the play's setting</li>
    <li>Discover literary traditions that influenced <i>Macbeth</i></li>
</ul>
